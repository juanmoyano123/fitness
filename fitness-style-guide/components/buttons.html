Usage Guidelines

Button Type When to Use Example
Primary Main call-to-action on a page or section "Start Workout", "Save Progress"
Secondary Supporting actions of medium importance "View Details", "Edit Program"
Outline Tertiary actions or navigation "Cancel", "Log In"
Accent Special promotional or achievement actions "Claim Reward", "Upgrade Now"
Full Width Mobile layouts or card-based designs "Continue", "Next Step"
